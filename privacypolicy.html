Privacy Policy

Namaste, and welcome to Harinaam. We deeply respect your privacy and the sacredness of your spiritual journey. This Privacy Policy is our promise to you about how we handle your personal information. It's written simply, because trust shouldn't be complicated.

1. What We Collect and Why

When you sign up, we ask for basic details like your name, email, or phone number. We only collect what we genuinely need to create your personal profile, track your daily chanting streaks, and provide you with personalized spiritual insights. We don't ask for unnecessary data.

2. Protecting Your Sacred Data

Your personal information, including your chanting progress and Krishna Chat history, is treated with the utmost care. We use industry-standard security measures (like Firebase's secure databases) to keep your data safe. We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your spiritual practice is between you and the Divine.

3. The Tools We Use (Third-Party Services)

To make the app run smoothly, we occasionally use trusted third-party tools (like Google Sign-In or Crashlytics to catch bugs). These services might collect anonymous log data if the app crashes, helping us fix issues faster. They are strictly bound to protect your data as well.

4. Your Choices and Control

This is your journey. You have the right to review the information we hold about you and request that we delete your account and data at any time. If you ever wish to step away, you can simply reach out to us, and we will gracefully remove your footprint from our servers.

5. Connecting With Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or how your data is handled, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are here to listen and help.

May you find peace and focus in your daily practice.
